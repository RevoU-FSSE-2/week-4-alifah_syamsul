Trivia

  1. Multilingual singer: Mika is fluent in English, French, Spanish, and Italian and releases music in these languages.
  2. Opera training: Mika studied classical music at the Royal College of Music, which is reflected in his wide vocal range.
  3. "Grace Kelly" inspiration: His hit single "Grace Kelly" was written in defiance of record companies wanting him to conform to other artists' styles.
  4. Illustration skills: Mika, an art school graduate, often creates his own album artwork.
  5. UN ambassador: Mika was made a UNHCR Goodwill Ambassador in 2020, advocating for refugee rights.
